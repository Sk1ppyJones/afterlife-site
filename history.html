December, 2014

Season 1 started on December 21, 2014 during Minecraft's 1.8 "The Bountiful Update"

March, 2015

The AfterLife Illuminati is discovered and being lead by AllOutJay

June, 2016

Season 2 started around June 15, 2016 during Minecraft's 1.9 "The Combat Update"

October, 2016

One of the first major events on the server is hosted with the AfterLife Purge won by the IronKoala's or HutchieLP & Dukester with 19 total kills

June, 2019

Season 3 started around June 22, 2019 during Minecraft's 1.14 "Village & Pillage Update" and was the longest actively running season until season 5!

January, 2020

BlondSkunk Founds Team Turtle, beginning the battle between them and Team Panda. Said battle would lead to a number of pranks and amazing builds expanding the server.

June, 2021

Season 4 started around June 16, 2021 during Minecraft's 1.16 update but right as 1.17 "Caves & Cliff" was released.

June, 2021

Ash'sDen builds the robot DRIPPO or the "Derelict Robot of Intensely Peaceful Protection Operations" meant to gradually take charge of the server, leading to a server war and the founding of teams to fight against DRIPPO or promote peace.

June, 2022

The fifth season of AfterLife started on June 11, 2022 with the realease of Minecraft's 1.19 "The Wild Update". This is the current season of AfterLife with a runtime of over 2 years!

May, 2024

TinkFro & Ash'sDen being the Gold Revolution, leading to the assasination of AllOutJay and the servers first election which is decided largely by the continous debate surrounding what currency should be used on the server.
